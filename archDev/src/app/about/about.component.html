Background

I was born and army brat and with a servants attitude. As soon as I could I joined the US Army Infantry and received several medals to include a Bronze Star with Valor and a Purple Heart during my service. On my last deployment to Ramadi, Iraq I was injured and subsequently honorably separated from service.

I used what I had learned in the service and attended college to become a paramedic. While continuing to serve my community I was asked to maintain the current website for the union I belonged to as well as build another website for a community event. I had always had an interest in development but after doing this I knew this would be my future. It started becoming increasingly difficult to preform my duties with my previous injuries so I decided I would follow my interest and become a developer.

I put in my notice, sold my house and moved my family to Ohio to attend Tech Elevator and start my future. I completed the Tech Elevator boot camp in Aug 2018. Since then I have been doing volunteer work on an app for the Hue Jackson Foundation in partnership with iFuse Solutions. Now it is time for me to get a job and learn even more.
